- **Description :** **Extensible Markup Language** (**XML**) is a [markup language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markup_language "Markup language") and [file format](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File_format "File format") for storing, transmitting, and reconstructing arbitrary data.
